**Conclusion**
**If Petal Length < 3 cm then the Species of the flower is Setosa.**

**If Petal Length >= 3 and Petal Length < 5 then most of the Species are Versicolor.**

**If Petal Length > 5 cm then most of the Species are Virginica.**
